CNP-A

Welcome to the Clinical Neuropsychology Assembly, an online community platform dedicated to bringing together professionals, researchers, and clinicians in the field of neuropsychology. Our Discord server provides a space for collaboration, knowledge sharing, and networking among peers in the neuropsychological community.

Research Focus

Our platform supports neuropsychological research through dedicated channels for methodology discussions, literature reviews, and collaborative research opportunities. Connect with fellow researchers to discuss current studies, share insights, and explore potential collaborations.

We regularly host journal clubs, research presentations, and discussions on emerging trends in neuropsychological assessment and intervention research.

Clinical Practice

For clinicians, our community offers a space to discuss case conceptualizations, assessment techniques, and intervention strategies in neuropsychology. Connect with experienced practitioners and share insights on best practices for various neurological conditions.

The platform provides resources for continuing education, supervision discussions, and updates on clinical guidelines in the field of neuropsychology.
